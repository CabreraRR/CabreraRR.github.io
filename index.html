About Us

Rothschild Resource Management Corp.

Rothschild is a full service Resource Management Corporation specializing in assisting corporations through, transition, transformation and growth in this constantly evolving marketplace.
We offer the following Services:

  • Administration
  • Construction
  • Engineering
  • Finance
  • Healthcare
  • IT
  • Marketing
  • Project Management

Services

Administration

Administration is an essential task that will help to ensure the proper operation of your business. Previously, efficiencies within a business' administration are often only obtainable to large organisations with a dedicated Admin Team, but this is no longer the case. With Rothschild Resource Management, organisations of any size are able to achieve substantial improvements to their processes.

Construction

Rothschild Resource Management offers comprehensive services designed allow an organization to finish a project. Some of these services include; design work, remodeling and alterations, permitting, site preparation, carpentry, cement foundations, painting, and plumbing and utilities installation.

Engineering

Engineering solutions are creative and innovative while maintaining a project’s profitability. Rothschild Resource Management helps organizations to maintain their competitive advantage, through an integrated strategy that facilitates continuous cost improvement across the product realization value stream, while accelerating revenue growth through innovation. Experienced engineers are available to work on projects and provide expert engineering solutions that will produce a first-rate outcome.

Finance

Experienced finance and accounting professionals have the ability to manage all of the financial needs of an organization. Rothschild Resource Management has experts in the financial field who will be able to assist in the monetary management and the process of acquiring needed funds.

Healthcare

A skilled team of healthcare professionals is ready to facilitate medical care. These healthcare professionals offer primary, secondary, tertiary, home, and community care while offering hospital, health plan, physician quality service. At Rothschild Resource Management, healthcare professionals are knowledgeable and experienced and prepared to encounter a large range of medical procedures.
