About Us

We offer the following Services:

  • Administration
  • Construction
  • Engineering
  • Finance
  • Healthcare
  • IT
  • Marketing
  • Project Management

Services

Administration

Administration is an essential task that will help to ensure the proper operation of your business. Previously, efficiencies within a business' administration are often only obtainable to large organisations with a dedicated Admin Team, but this is no longer the case. With Rothschild Resource Management, organisations of any size are able to achieve substantial improvements to their processes.

Construction

Rothschild Resource Management offers comprehensive services designed allow an organization to finish a project. Some of these services include; design work, remodeling and alterations, permitting, site preparation, carpentry, cement foundations, painting, and plumbing and utilities installation.

Engineering

Engineering solutions are creative and innovative while maintaining a project’s profitability. Rothschild Resource Management helps organizations to maintain their competitive advantage, through an integrated strategy that facilitates continuous cost improvement across the product realization value stream, while accelerating revenue growth through innovation. Experienced engineers are available to work on projects and provide expert engineering solutions that will produce a first-rate outcome.

Finance

Experienced finance and accounting professionals have the ability to manage all of the financial needs of an organization. Rothschild Resource Management has experts in the financial field who will be able to assist in the monetary management and the process of acquiring needed funds.

Healthcare

A skilled team of healthcare professionals is ready to facilitate medical care. These healthcare professionals offer primary, secondary, tertiary, home, and community care while offering hospital, health plan, physician quality service. At Rothschild Resource Management, healthcare professionals are knowledgeable and experienced and prepared to encounter a large range of medical procedures.

Information Technology

IT professionals are certified and experienced in storing, retrieving, transmitting, and manipulating data, or information. Specialties ranging from computer hardware, software, electronics, semiconductors, internet, telecom equipment, and e-commerce are available to fit an organization’s need. Rothschild Resource Management can offer IT specialists with the proper training and certification to see your project to fruition.

Marketing

Marketing professionals promote an organization’s product or service for customers, clients, partners, and society at large. At Rothschild Resource Management, experienced marketing professionals are able to utilize digital marketing strategies to ensure maximum exposure.

Project Management

Project managers are accountable for the outcome of a project. Project managers are responsible for managing teams and expectations along with planning, executing and closing projects. At Rothschild Resource Management, experienced project managers are prepared to lead a team to a project’s completion. These project managers can apply various knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to project activities to meet the project requirements.

Marketing Professionals

Experienced marketing professionals forge a plan of action to increase the exposure of an organization’s product or service. The marketing strategy is derived from the overall business strategy, and marketing professionals utilize this situation to devise strategic activities for promotion. At Rothschild Resource Management, marketing professionals are able to adapt to any situation. These marketing professionals are able to formulate a marketing strategy from its origin or assist and develop an existing marketing strategy.

Special Services

Infection Control

Every year, there is a loss of life due to infection spread within a medical environment. Infection control specialists have the training needed to advance hospital procedures to prevent the spread of infectious diseases. At Rothschild Resource Management, infection control specialists utilize state-of-the-art technologies to prevent the spread of infection. Infection control specialists advance procedures to align with a growing medical field.

Environment, Health and Safety

Environment, health and safety (EHS) professionals are experienced and trained to comply with regulations to ensure procedures are in place to prevent harm towards the environment or to anyone. EHS professionals create an organized efforts and procedures to identify workplace hazards and reducing accidents and exposure to harmful situations and substances. At Rothschild Resource Management, EHS professionals are trained and experienced with safety as their primary objective.
